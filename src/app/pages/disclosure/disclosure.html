Our Affiliate Model

In compliance with the FTC Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising (16 CFR Part 255), we declare that betterthanthebasic operates as a commercial, reader-supported blog.

This means that throughout our blueprints, guides, and articles:
- We include special tracking links (affiliate links) to various products, gear, books, and financial services.
- If you click on one of these links and complete a purchase, the merchant network pays us a small percentage (commission) of the transaction value.
- This commission is paid entirely by the merchant. The retail price of the item is exactly the same for you whether you use our link or search for it directly.

Why We Use This Model

Detailed research, professional testing, technical specifications comparison, and web server maintenance require substantial resources. The affiliate model enables us to offer all our premium blueprints to readers for free, without cluttering the site with intrusive pop-up banners or putting up paywalls.

Our Honesty Guarantee

We do not accept direct payment from brands to write positive ratings. We do not participate in "pay-to-play" schemes. If a product has a major drawback, we will list it. Our goal is to highlight premium gear that actually helps you elevate your defaults, not to generate quick sales of low-quality items.
